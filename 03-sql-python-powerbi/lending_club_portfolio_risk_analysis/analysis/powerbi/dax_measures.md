# DAX Measures Documentation

This document describes all DAX measures used in the Power BI dashboard **Lending Club Portfolio Risk & Profitability Analysis**.
All measures are aligned with the business questions and insights identified during the SQL analysis phase.

---
## =========================

## English Version

## =========================
---

## 1. Total Loans

**Description**
Counts the total number of loans in the portfolio.

**DAX**

Total Loans = COUNT(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_id])

**Business Logic**
- Measures total portfolio size
- Used as a primary exposure indicator
- Supports segmentation by term and credit score

---

## 2. Capital Deployed

**Description**
Calculates the total capital deployed across all loans.

**DAX**

Total Capital = SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_amount])

**Business Logic**
- Represents total financial exposure
- Used to evaluate capital allocation
- Serves as denominator for ROI calculations

---

## 3. Default Rate

**Description**
Measures the percentage of loans that resulted in default (Charged Off).

**DAX**

Default Rate = 
    DIVIDE(
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[is_default]),
        COUNT(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_id])
    )

**Business Logic**
- is_default = 1 when loan status = Charged Off
- Captures realized credit risk
- Core KPI for portfolio risk evaluation

---

## 4. Late Rate

**Description**
Measures the percentage of loans currently in early delinquency (Late).

**DAX**

Late Rate = 
    DIVIDE(
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[is_late]),
        COUNT(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_id])
    )

**Business Logic**
- is_late = 1 when loan status = Late
- Indicates early portfolio deterioration
- Supports proactive risk monitoring

---

## 5. Portfolio ROI (%)

**Description**
Calculates the portfolio-level return on investment based on interest received over capital deployed.

**DAX**

Portfolio ROI = 
    DIVIDE(
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[interest_paid]),
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_amount])
    )

**Business Logic**
- Measures real return generated by lending activity
- Accounts for total interest received
- Allows risk-return comparison across segments

## 1. Total de Préstamos

**Descripción**
Cuenta el número total de préstamos en el portafolio.

**DAX**

Total Loans = COUNT(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_id])

**Lógica de Negocio**
- Mide el tamaño del portafolio
- Funciona como indicador principal de exposición
- Permite segmentación por plazo y credit score

---

## 2. Capital Total

**Descripción**
Calcula el capital total colocado en todos los préstamos.

**DAX**

Total Capital = SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_amount])

**Lógica de Negocio**
- Representa la exposición financiera total
- Permite evaluar asignación de capital
- Sirve como base para el cálculo del ROI

---

## 3. Tasa de Default

**Descripción**
Mide el porcentaje de préstamos que terminaron en incumplimiento (Charged Off).

**DAX**

Default Rate = 
    DIVIDE(
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[is_default]),
        COUNT(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_id])
    )

**Lógica de Negocio**
- is_default = 1 cuando el estado es Charged Off
- Captura el riesgo crediticio realizado
- KPI central para evaluar riesgo del portafolio

---

## 4. Tasa de Mora

**Descripción**
Mide el porcentaje de préstamos en estado de mora temprana (Late).

**DAX**

Late Rate = 
    DIVIDE(
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[is_late]),
        COUNT(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_id])
    )

**Lógica de Negocio**
- is_late = 1 cuando el estado es Late
- Indica deterioro temprano del portafolio
- Permite monitoreo preventivo del riesgo

---

## 5. ROI del Portafolio

**Descripción**
Calcula el retorno total del portafolio basado en el interés recibido sobre el capital colocado.

**DAX**

Portfolio ROI = 
    DIVIDE(
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[interest_paid]),
        SUM(loan_analysis_dataset[loan_amount])
    )

**Lógica de Negocio**
- Mide la rentabilidad real generada por la actividad crediticia
- Considera el interés efectivamente recibido
- Permite comparar riesgo y rentabilidad entre segmentos
